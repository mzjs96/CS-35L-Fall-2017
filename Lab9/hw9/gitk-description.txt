Top left window: This window shows a hierarchical structure of commits. It
displays a visual representation of the history of commits in the form of a
tree, showing different branches, relationships, the head pointer, and commit
messages.

Top middle window: This window displays corresponding authors and their emails
associated with each of the commits displayed in the commit history tree.

Top right window: This window shows the dates and times of all the commits
displayed by the same history tree.

Bottom left window: This window shows the contents of the ChangeLog of the
currently selected commit in a diff format. The ChangeLog contains information
about the commit, such as committer, author, parent, child, branches, etc.

Bottom right window: This window lists the files directly changed and modified
by the selected commit.
